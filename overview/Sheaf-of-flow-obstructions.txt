Shaf S: Rigidity-Fields Line-Bind Structures

in the stationary class. Each sU is indexed by its stanchion-mark, sU = ⃗σ(U), piecewise-constant
across the binding corridors, jumping on approach to the queue-walls.
The overlap law reads:

forced travelers through unidirectional compression.
